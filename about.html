About Me

A passionate 3D Artist mastering the complete ecosystem of Environment Creation, from terrain to vegetation.

Creative 3D Environment Artist

Hi, I'm Ahmed - turning artistic concepts into living, breathing digital worlds.

I am a 3D Generalist with a deep focus on Organic World-Building. I don't just place assets; I sculpt the land itself.

My workflow integrates the full power of nature creation tools. I generate realistic terrains using Gaea and Houdini Heightfields, and populate them with custom vegetation from SpeedTree. Combined with my skills in Layout and Set Dressing, I deliver cohesive, visually stunning environments that tell a story.
